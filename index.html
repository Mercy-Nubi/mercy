About Me

Hi, I'm Mercy Nubi. I'm the Founder of AVERSE Studios, a creative technology studio helping brands and creators bring bold ideas to life through AI, motion design, filmmaking, and digital product experiences.

I believe great edit is about telling stories that people remember and building products that solve real problems. Whether I'm designing interfaces, creating cinematic motion graphics, producing AI powered content, or exploring emerging creative tools, my goal is always the same, create work that moves people.

My background as both a Product and Motion Designer and a Doctor of Optometry has shaped how I approach every project. One taught me to understand people deeply, while the other taught me to communicate ideas with clarity, precision, and creativity.

I'm particularly passionate about the future of AI in the creative industry. I enjoy experimenting with new technologies, simplifying complex ideas, and helping businesses leverage AI to build faster, market smarter, and tell better stories.

When I'm not designing or creating, you'll probably find me researching the latest AI breakthroughs, developing new creative workflows, or sharing insights that help other creators stay ahead of what's next.

If you're building something ambitious, I'd love to hear about it.
